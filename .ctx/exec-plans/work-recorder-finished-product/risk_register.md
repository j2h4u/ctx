# Work Recorder Finished Product Risk Register

## Open Risks

- Provider real E2E runs may depend on locally available Codex, Claude, and Pi installations and credentials.
- Public binary publication and public installer URLs may require a deliberate release decision separate from release dry-run certification.
- Hosted/team sync is out of public launch scope unless explicitly promoted later.
- Real GitHub PR publishing smoke should use a private scratch repository and may depend on available auth.
